Who am I?

I was born in Poznań in 1995 and with my creative and fast learning abilities, I have transferred my knowledge from being a physicist towards the path of a frontend developer. I have worked on numerous research and development projects associated with both academic and commercial sectors, however I have always felt my place was elsewhere. In my youth, I have paid a lot of attention to art, a sense of aesthetics, and the basics of design. Currently, I have decided to use my previous experience and start of a new path of development. In 2021, I started postgraduate studies in web development in order to combine all this knowledge.
